# Sample Bank Policy Document

## GENERAL BANKING POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

### Account Opening Requirements
All new account holders must provide valid government-issued photo identification and proof of address. Minimum opening deposits are $25 for savings accounts and $100 for checking accounts.

### Fee Structure
- Monthly maintenance fee: $10 for checking accounts (waived with $1,500 minimum balance)
- ATM fees: $2.50 for out-of-network transactions
- Overdraft fees: $35 per transaction
- Wire transfer fees: $25 domestic, $45 international
- Stop payment fees: $30
- Paper statement fees: $5 per month (waived for online statements)

### Account Closure Policy
Accounts may be closed at any time with written notice. Final statements will be mailed within 30 days. Outstanding fees and charges must be paid before closure.

### Privacy Policy
We protect your personal information and do not sell customer data to third parties. Information may be shared with affiliated companies and service providers as necessary for account management.

### Dispute Resolution
Customers have 60 days from statement date to report unauthorized transactions or errors. We will investigate and provide provisional credit within 10 business days for qualifying disputes.

### Online Banking Security
- Use strong passwords with at least 8 characters
- Never share login credentials
- Log out completely when finished
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- We will never ask for passwords via email or phone

### Hours of Operation
- Branch hours: Monday-Friday 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Saturday 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- Customer service: Monday-Friday 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM, Saturday-Sunday 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
- Online banking: Available 24/7 with scheduled maintenance Sundays 1:00 AM - 4:00 AM

### Loan Policies
Personal loans available from $1,000 to $50,000 with terms up to 5 years. Credit approval required. Interest rates vary based on creditworthiness and loan term.

### CD Rates and Terms
Certificate of Deposit rates vary by term length:
- 6 months: 2.50% APY
- 1 year: 3.00% APY
- 2 years: 3.25% APY
- 5 years: 3.75% APY
Minimum deposit $1,000. Early withdrawal penalties apply.

### International Services
We offer international wire transfers, foreign currency exchange, and travel notifications for debit/credit card usage abroad. Fees apply for international services.
