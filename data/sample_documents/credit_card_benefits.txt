# Credit Card Benefits Guide

## PREMIUM REWARDS CREDIT CARD

- Earn 2X points on all purchases
- 3X points on gas and groceries
- 5X points on rotating quarterly categories
- Points never expire
- Redeem points for cash back, travel, or merchandise

### Welcome Bonus
Earn 50,000 bonus points after spending $3,000 in the first 3 months. That's $500 in cash back!

$95 annual fee (waived first year for new cardholders)

- Extended warranty: Extends manufacturer warranty by up to 1 additional year

### Travel Benefits
- No foreign transaction fees
- Travel accident insurance up to $500,000
- Rental car insurance coverage
- Trip cancellation/interruption insurance up to $5,000
- 24/7 travel assistance services

### Security Features
- $0 fraud liability protection
- Real-time fraud monitoring

- Virtual card numbers for online shopping
- EMV chip technology

## GENERAL CREDIT CARD POLICIES

### Payment Information
- Minimum payment due: Greater of $35 or 1% of balance
- Payment due date: Same date each month
- Grace period: 25 days on purchases (when previous balance paid in full)
- Late payment fee: Up to $40

### Interest Rates (APR)
- Premium Rewards: 16.99% - 24.99% Variable APR
- Gold Cash Back: 15.99% - 23.99% Variable APR  
- Student Card: 17.99% - 25.99% Variable APR
- Cash advance APR: 27.99% Variable APR (all cards)

### Credit Limit Management
- Credit limit increases available after 6 months of responsible use
- Automatic reviews for limit increases
- Temporary limit increases available for special circumstances
- Real-time balance and available credit tracking

### Dispute Resolution
- 60 days to report billing errors or unauthorized charges
- Provisional credit provided during investigation
- Zero liability for fraud when reported promptly
- Dedicated fraud resolution specialists
